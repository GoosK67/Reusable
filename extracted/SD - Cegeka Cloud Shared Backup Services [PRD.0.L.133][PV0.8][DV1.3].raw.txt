Service Description
Service Summary
This section gives a brief summary of the described Service. 

The Service covers all tasks and activities required to deliver the Backup & Recovery Service (also referred to as 'Backup-as-a-Service'). Through this managed service offering, Cegeka aims to relieve the Customer of the technical responsibilities associated with managing and operating a backup platform.

The standard Service features are designed to ensure the platform supporting the Backup & Recovery Service remains operational, secure, and technically optimized.


Services
Cegeka’s Backup & Recovery Service provides comprehensive protection and recovery capabilities for systems and data hosted in Cegeka datacenters, as well as on satellite locations such as public cloud environments and customer-operated datacenters or datarooms. 

The service is built on the Commvault backup and recovery software platform and operates on Cegeka-owned, secured, and isolated infrastructure. It includes the full management of backup processes and the secure storage of backup data. The service functions independently from all other Cegeka services to ensure the highest level of availability.

Security and Compliance
Security and compliance are central to any Service delivered by Cegeka. Cegeka ensures that all infrastructure components are deployed, configured, and maintained in line with recognized security standards and agreed contractual requirements. The goal is to protect the infrastructure component and its data while aligning with both Cegeka’s internal security practices and the Customer’s specific policies.

For this particular Service, the supporting platform -based on Commvault technology- has been configured follwing the vendor's security guidelines, which outlines and support Center for Internet Security (CIS) Level 1 benchmarks for hardening.

Security incident management is performed in accordance with Cegeka’s internal policies as well as the contractual security requirements of the Customer. When a potential breach is detected, a security incident ticket is created and communicated without delay to both the Customer and the relevant Cegeka stakeholders.

The infrastructure components (both hardware and software) that make up the Backup & Recovery Service platform, are by default monitored through Cegeka's Monitoring Platform. Optional Services
This section outlines the optional services which may be added to the list of standard services and -when applicable- will be priced in addition to the standard offering.

The following options can be enabled, yet require a technical review prior to activation:
Disk to Tape offloading*: This is a service extension and must be activated separately. Tape offloading ensures that customer data is written to dedicated/encrypted tapes and transported to a secure offsite facility, providing an additional layer of protection for information that must be preserved over extended periods. This method is designed exclusively for storing data with long-term retention requirements.

Threat Scanning: This feature is not enabled by default on the platform. Threat Scanning addresses a critical weakness in traditional restore processes, where malware or ransomware can be unintentionally reintroduced into clean environments, leading to costly delays and repeated outages. By scanning backup data for indicators of compromise before recovery begins, Threat Scanning ensures that only clean, verified workloads are restored. 
 
When activated for customers who also use Cegeka’s Modern SOC services, Threat Scanning is seamlessly integrated to provide enhanced analytical context and to help ensure that any required response is executed as effectively as possible.

Air-gapped Tape Vault*: This is a service extension and must be activated separately. The Air-gapped Tape Vault ensures that a copy of all protected workloads is retained for seven days on encrypted tapes, which are stored in the vault immediately after they are written. This creates an offline, inaccessible copy of the most recent backups and significantly enhances data security.

Microsoft 365 backup: This optional service strengthens Microsoft 365's native protection by storing copies outside Microsoft Azure with near-infinite** retention. It delivers independent, secure, and continuous data protection, ensuring recovery from accidental deletion, cyberattacks, or outages in a SaaS environment. The service covers data from Exchange Online, SharePoint Online (including Project Online), OneDrive for Business, and Teams.

Satellite Backup: This is a service extension and must be activated separately. Satellite Backup safeguards satellite locations, covering public cloud environments and customer-managed datacenters or data rooms. It offers complete protection for workloads wherever they are, using a fully managed service to maintain isolation, security, immutability, and regulatory-compliant protection operations.

Activation of any optional technical features or service extensions requires the creation of an RFC, which will be handled in accordance with the standard change process. Additionally, the Backup Plan will be updated to reflect the changes introduced by these new features and services. 

* Both service extensions, Tape to Disk Offloading and Air-gapped Tape Vault, are available only to customers who are already onboarded into the Backup & Recovery Service. Access to these extensions requires consumption of the core service and they cannot be offered as standalone solutions.
** Near-infinite retention for Microsoft 365 backup, guarantees a 10 year retention. In case of GDPR concerns, retention can be lowered to 1 month. A fair use policy applies with regards to storage consumption: Cegeka does not impose hard limits on storage utilization but reserves the right to charge additional costs if utilization is disproportionate against common industry average and/or averages across our install base.


Service Requests 
The handling of requests -either standard or non-standard- is subject to the regular change management process (see Enabling Services).


Standard Change Requests
Standard change requests related to the Service, can be requested when available.  

A standard change request is a pre-approved and pre-defined service request, often used for recurring needs that arise multiple times per month. All standard change requests are processed as change tickets and are delivered within the indicated lead time. Wherever possible, the execution of these requests is optimally automated, ensuring efficiency and consistency. Standard change requests are offered at a fixed price.

* estimated lead time applies from the moment the change request has been received by the delivery team.


Non-standard Change Requests
Next to Standard Change Requests, the Service also facilitates and supports non-standard change requests when applicable.

Non-standard change requests are requests which are not pre-approved nor pre-defined and therefore require a thorough evaluation and analysis. In such cases, Cegeka prepares a proposal along with an impact assessment and a time-and-materials (T&M) estimation to address the request. Before execution, the proposal must undergo review, approval, and planning by the Change Advisory Board (CAB), including the scheduling of an appropriate maintenance window. Non-standard change requests are billed on a T&M basis.

Prerequisites & Conditions 
This section defines the conditions that must be fulfilled for Cegeka to deliver the described Service effectively. 

Prerequisites : 
The backup software requires full administrative access to any system included within the defined backup scope. Cegeka must therefore be authorised to install and configure this software on the relevant systems. While the software performs the backup and restore operations, only the designated Cegeka service team is permitted to initiate these processes.
Any system within the defined backup scope must be supported and compatible with the backup software employed. (for more detailed information about supported systems, please refer to Commvault’s official technical documentation) 
Service extensions such as 'Disk to Tape offloading' or 'Air-gapped Tape Vault' are available only to customers who are already onboarded into the Backup & Recovery Service. Access to these extensions requires consumption of the core service and they cannot be offered as standalone solutions.

Conditions :
All restore operations require a suitable target environment. The setup of this environment is not part of the standard service offering; however, it can be delivered on request and is billed on a time-and-material basis.
Cegeka recommends performing regular data and system restore tests to validate the backup and restore strategy and its effectiveness. These tests are processed as standard restore requests and must be initiated by the Customer through the RFC process or by establishing and approving a project letter. All associated efforts are billed on a time-and-materials basis.
Upon activation of the 'Disk to Tape offloading' service extension, the 'Secure Tape Storage'- service is automatically enabled as well. This service includes transferring the backup tapes to the operations team for shipment to the secure storage facility.
