Standard Service Description

Introduction

The My Meeting Room service offers a solution to enhance the organization’s collaboration and communication capabilities. This service is designed to streamline Meeting Rooms, improve productivity and create a seamless experience for both in-person and remote participants. With modern devices, technologies and integrated features, the service aims to transform your Meeting Rooms into modern, efficient and collaborative spaces. 

The service assumes the use of a central management platform for a Meeting Room.

What is the added business value?

The My Meeting Room service aims to standardize and streamline your Meeting Room experience by focussing on key features such as high quality audio & video, seamless connectivity, interactive displays, wireless content sharing and room scheduling & management all while doing this in a way to ensure security and privacy.

The solution is designed to empower organizations with modern collaboration tools that foster innovation, efficiency and effective communication. By leveraging the aforementioned key features, our service transforms Meeting Rooms into hubs of productivity and creativity, whether participants are gathered in person or attending remotely.

What is included in the service?

Our Meeting Room service contains the following minimum set of devices and software:

  • Display
  • Audio (Microphone/Speakers)
  • Video (Camera)
  • Control (Touch display for interacting with the Meeting Room)
  • Video input

Note that these functionalities are not always realized by separate devices as many of them can be combined into multipurpose devices (e.g. soundbar with built-in camera, all-in-one solution, etc.).

Small Meeting Room

The Small Meeting Room consists of the following components:

    • Display: Large TV or monitor.
    • Audio: Speakers or soundbar.
    • Video: Webcam or conference camera.
    • Control: Dedicated control panel and/or touchscreen interface.
    • Connectivity: Wired and/or wireless connectivity options (HDMI, USB-C, AirPlay, Miracast).
    • Meeting Room OS: Android/Windows OS designed to combine all the above components into a functional Meeting Room.

The Small Meeting Room:

  • can host 3-5 people
  • has a surface area of +/- 14 m²
Medium Meeting Room

The Medium Meeting Room consists of the following components:

The Medium Meeting Room:

  • has a surface area of +/- 28 m²
Large Meeting Room

The large Meeting Room consists of the following components:

    • Display: Large TV or monitor.

Collaborative Meeting Room

The collaborative Meeting Room consists of the following components:

The Collaborative Meeting Room:

Platform management

The Platform Management Service contains following management platforms

  • Central Meeting Room management platform
  • Device Management platform (manage Meeting Room configurations)
  • Cloud based mail platform (manage Meeting Room mailboxes)

which allow for the Management of the Platform (configuration and analysis):

    • Platform management: 
      • Maintain & support the management platforms (Central Meeting Room management platform, Device Management platform & Cloud based mail platform) 
    • Proactive monitoring
      • Acting on alerts in the platform, support the platform if a Meeting Room related platform service is down or degraded, including communication with the vendor and customer
    • Maintain & support the configured Compliance, Configuration and device restrictions Policies
      • Maintain the existing policies and keep them in line with our evolving standards

The following services are considered platform services as they are generally performed for most/all devices simultaneously:

  • Management of software
    • Always up-to-date
      • Patching of control software (based on release schedule of vendor)
      • Patching of managed Meeting Room hardware device OS (monthly or based on release schedule and recommendations of vendor)
      • Upgrade of managed Meeting Room hardware device OS (based on release schedule and recommendations of vendor)
    • Integrated security
      • Antivirus:
        • Update the antivirus definitions of managed Meeting Room hardware device OS based on release schedule of vendor (if applicable)
  • Reporting
    • Monthly reporting of following parameters per active Meeting Room in scope (where applicable)
      • OS patch level
      • Meeting Room software version
      • Antivirus definition level
      • Antivirus state
      • Detected malware
      • Encryption state
      • Compliance
  • Management of the drivers/firmware of the managed Meeting Room hardware devices
    • Review and update of drivers/firmware when made available by vendors.

Support

Cegeka applies following Support flux:

  • Customer contacts 1st line support
    • If resolved, incident is closed
    • If not resolved, (Cegeka) Service Desk transfers the incident ticket to Cegeka MR Software Management Resolver Group who provides Remote Support only
      • If resolved, incident is closed
      • If not resolved, Cegeka MR Software Management Resolver Group transfers the incident ticket to MR HW Management Resolver Group (Customer’s own support people or Cegeka’s existing on-site support people or Cegeka’s optional on-site support people (if not existing on-site support))
        • If resolved, incident is closed
        • If not resolved, MR HW Management Resolver Group transfers the incident ticket to the Cegeka Field Support swap team that performs a Break-Fix activity
          • Incident can be resolved if hardware is still under vendor warranty
          • Offer will be made by Cegeka or a new device has to be acquired by the Customer if hardware is not under vendor warranty

Measurement

The monthly price of the Meeting Room service is based upon a Unit price (P) for following

  • Standard service components
    • The software management per Meeting Room
    • The hardware lifecycle management with corresponding Warranty Pack per Meeting Room
    • The central Meeting Room management platform service for all Meeting Rooms.
  • Optional service components
    • The management of a Physical Booking System

and the quantity of Meeting Rooms, Physical Booking System (Q). The corresponding Unit of Measurement is mentioned in Table above.

Definition for central Meeting Room management platform and Meeting Room are mentioned in Section ‘Conditions’.

[(software management per Meeting Room)*(Quantity of Meeting Rooms) + (HCLM Warranty Pack)*( Quantity of Meeting Rooms per Warranty Pack) + (Platform cost)]+[(management Physical Booking System)*(Quantity of Physical Booking Systems)]+ )]

Conditions

  • The Cegeka Service Desk should be in place.
  • The Service requires an instance of the Microsoft 365 SaaS platform component Teams and the necessary license per Meeting Room for Microsoft Teams.
  • Meeting Room hardware needs to be Microsoft Teams Certified.
  • The necessary licenses (Microsoft Teams Rooms Basic or Pro license) are available and assigned to the Meeting Rooms.
  • Cegeka has full access to Teams via the “Teams Administrator” role.
  • Cegeka has full access to Intune via the “Intune Administrator” role (for creation and management of Meeting Room device configuration profiles).
  • Cegeka has full access to Exchange Online via the “Exchange Online Administrator” role.
  • The Meeting Room software is the Microsoft Teams Rooms application
  • The cloud based mail platform needs to be Exchange Online.
  • The device management platform needs to be Intune.
  • The central Meeting Room management platform needs to be Teams.
  • The necessary network configuration is in place to allow communication between the Meeting Room devices and the necessary Microsoft services for full functionality.
  • For the Hardware Lifecycle Management Services Cegeka refers to the Service Description for “Device Lifecycle Management Services”.
  • Cegeka is brand-independent and works closely together with Poly, Logitech, Cisco, Lenovo, Microsoft and other manufacturers.
  • A Meeting Room is defined as the hardware/software components listed under the standard hardware lifecycle management and software services, more specifically under ‘Meeting Room types’.

Out of scope

  • The necessary structural works to enable Meeting Room setup, such as (but not limited to) cabling, wall mounting brackets, electrical wiring, furniture, etc.
  • Support for non-Teams Certified setups.
  • The physical components like the actual physical building (ceiling, walls, floor)/room and equipment like furniture, power outlets, network connectivity (wifi/LAN), telephony connection, HVAC, coffee and other beverages machines, fire detection systems, refrigerators and other kitchen equipment, lighting, curtains, carpet, whiteboards are out of scope.
  • Handling of Service related incidents by the Cegeka Service Desk (L1 support activities)
