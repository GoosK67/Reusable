Security & Networking

Site-to-Site VPN

Service summary

The Cegeka offering includes Site-to-Site connectivity for customers or third parties towards the customer networks in the datacenter based on IPSec connections over the Internet (or when required, over WAN connections). Depending on the type of VPN tunnel and the number of tunnels required, Cegeka will either host the tunnel end-point on the customer dedicated virtual firewall instance or on separate VPN end-point hardware.

Both Policy Based and Route Based VPNs are supported. Note that with route based VPNs, it is possible to create high-available VPN connections (by means of secondary VPN connections that can be automatically failed over to in case of primary connection failure) and also to use these as backup to a primary (routed) WAN connection.

Standard service components

Cegeka shall be responsible in providing the following Services:

  • Management of the VPN end-point hardware in the Cegeka datacenters
  • Configuration and maintenance of the Cegeka side of the VPN tunnel
