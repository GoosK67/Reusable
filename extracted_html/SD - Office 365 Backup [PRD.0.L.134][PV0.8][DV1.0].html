Office 365 Backup

Service summary

Cegeka provides <klant> with an Office 365 Back-up solution. The Cegeka Shared Backup is powered by Commvault. It includes data back-up of: Outlook 365, OneDrive for Business, SharePoint online and Microsoft Teams. By implementing Cegeka Shared Backup:

  • Data remains accessible even when O365 is offline;
  • Improved search capabilities are put in place;
  • Improved resiliency against cyber threats is ensured;
  • <klant> avoids cloud lock-in;
  • The native O365 protection is extended and expanded.

Standard service components

Cegeka shall be responsible in providing backup service for the following Office 365 Services: 

  • Microsoft 365 – Mail & Calendar
    • Infinite retention of mail *
    • Self-service restore 
  • Microsoft 365 - OneDrive for Business
    • Infinite retention of data **
    • Self-service restore 
  • Microsoft 365 - SharePoint online
    • Full & incremental backup schedule
    • Self-service restore
  • Microsoft 365 - Teams
    • Team conversations as stored in O365 mailbox(hidden folder) backup***
    • Team SharePoint site and document library backup
    • Wiki page library backup
    • Self-service restore

* Fair use policy – see service description

** Fair use policy – see service description. Deleted files kept indefinitely

*** In-place restores into Teams is not possible. (Microsoft limitation)

Eligibility and Prerequisites

Conditions

  • Microsoft 365 Teams backup requires Exchange and SharePoint backup to be set-up.

Out of scope

  • The packaging, patching and updating of applications are not in scope of this Service;
  • Licensing, support and patching of software published through this Service is not in scope of this Service;
  • This service does not include the related Cloud & on-premise infra resource consumption;
  • Activities that are related to replacing out-of-support or obsolete hardware are not in scope of this Service.
